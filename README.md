# Naviotech_Project11_Big_Mart_Sales_Prediction_ML_Project
This repository consist of the Big Mart Sales Prediction Project which predicts the total sales of the products based on several features. It basically predicts the total sale of any item in the overall stores of the Big Mart. 

Here basically, the prediction of the item cost at the outlet is made by considering the several features like the:  

1. Item_Weight 
2. Item_Fat_Content 
3. Item_Visibility 
4. item_Type  
5. Item_MRP 
6. Outlet Establishmnet Year 
7. Outlet_Size 
8. Outlet_Location_Type 
9. Outlet_Type

The algorithm being used here is the Random Forest Regressor which makes the predictions based on the combination of the decision trees and hence the predictions are made.
